<h2>What Are Cookies?</h2>

<p>Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, or other device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.</p>

<p>Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. This helps improve your browsing experience by:</p>
